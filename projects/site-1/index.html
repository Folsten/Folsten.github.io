Account details will be sent automatically to your email address instantly after purchase.
I got my account details, what's next?
Some accounts have unverified email, that means they don't have current email which attached to the purchased account, as result all you need to do is just login on this account via Riot's website and attach your own email to the account and after that change the password. All data you need in this case that's just login and password, which will be sent to you instantly after purchase.

Some of them with verified emails, that means they have current email attached to the account, as result such accounts will be delivered to you with account access + email access, which currently attached to the account, so you could change email to your own.

No worries, you can pay with absolutely any currency, paypal will make currency conversion automatically during the payment, no any additional actions required from you, just follow few default simple checkout steps.
